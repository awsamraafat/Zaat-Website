What is an FAQ section?

+

An FAQ section can be used to quickly answer common questions about your business like "Where do you ship to?", "What are your opening hours?", or "How can I book a service?".

Why do FAQs matter?

+

FAQs are a great way to help site visitors find quick answers to common questions about your business and create a better navigation experience.

Where can I add my FAQs?

+

You can add FAQs to any page on your site or to your mobile app, making them accessible to your users wherever they are.

How do I add a new question & answer?

+

Go to your app dashboard, click on FAQs, and select "Add New". Type in your question and answer, then click save.

Yes! You can enrich your FAQ answers by adding rich media assets directly in the editor.

+
