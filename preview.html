Welcome to Zaat

Zaat helps you share your professional identity instantly through smart NFC products, QR codes, and a customizable digital profile.

Whether you're using a Zaat Card, Keychain, Sticker, or Stand, this Help Center will guide you through setup, customization, troubleshooting, and account management.

What is Zaat?

+
Zaat is a smart networking platform that allows you to share your contact information, social media profiles, business details, payment links, and digital content through a single personalized profile.

Your profile can be shared using:
• NFC Products
• QR Codes
• Direct Profile Links

Instead of carrying paper business cards, Zaat allows you to update your information anytime without reprinting anything.

How Do I Create My Profile?

+
1. Download the Zaat App.
2. Create a new account.
3. Verify your email address.
4. Choose your unique username.
5. Add your profile information.
6. Upload your profile image.
7. Add your social and professional links.

Your profile becomes available immediately after setup.

What Is My Username?

+
Your username creates your public profile URL.

Example:
zaatlink.com/johnsmith

Choose a professional and memorable username because it becomes part of your public identity.

What Is An NFC Product?

+
NFC stands for Near Field Communication.

Every Zaat product contains a smart NFC chip that allows people to access your profile simply by tapping their phone against the product.

Available products include:
• NFC Cards
• Keychains
• Stickers
• Desk Stands

No application is required for visitors.

How Do I Activate My Product?

+
1. Open the Zaat App.
2. Navigate to Product Activation.
3. Tap Activate Product.
4. Hold your phone near the NFC product.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions.

Once activation is complete, the product will automatically connect to your profile.

Can I Change The Profile Linked To My Product?

+
Yes.

You can reconnect your product to another profile at any time from the Product Management section inside the app.

NFC Is Not Working

+
If your NFC product does not respond:
• Ensure NFC is enabled on your phone.
• Remove thick phone cases.
• Hold the phone near the center of the NFC product.
• Try another compatible device.
• Update the Zaat App.

If the issue continues, contact Support.

What Is My QR Code?

+
Your QR code is a scannable version of your profile link.

Anyone can scan it using their phone camera and instantly access your profile.
